Privacy Policy

Last modified: January 20, 2024

1. Privacy Policy of the RACQUET-LINK S.L. website

The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to inform you about how Racquet-Link S.L. ("Racquet-Link" or "We") collects, uses, and discloses personal information, and to explain your rights and choices regarding the personal information we collect about you when you visit our website. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom (UK) or Switzerland, the controller of your personal data is Racquet-Link S.L., located at Calle Alcalá 128 – Oficina 3 – Escalera Interna and CIF B56667231. This company is responsible for determining how and why your personal data will be processed in the context of your contractual and professional relationship with us.

This Privacy Policy applies to the personal information we collect and process on the Racquet-Link website Racquet-Link (“Website”) and in connection with the services we provide (“Services”). For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, your personal information means any information that Racquet-Link holds or obtains about you from which you can be personally identified, either directly or indirectly.


2. What personal information do we collect?

2.1.  We collect personal data from coaches or candidates interested in finding a job opportunity in the racquet sports industry, as well as from representatives or contact persons of sports clubs or institutions interested in hiring coaches. We collect personal information directly from the interested parties themselves and indirectly from third parties from whom, in certain processes, we ask for references to corroborate the information collected. The type of personal information we collect depends on the nature of our relationship with you (candidate or coach), but in all cases the categories are detailed below. You should note that if you do not provide us with certain personal information, we may not be able to provide you with the Services.

2.2.  Racquet-Link collects personal information about the following categories of individuals:

(a) individuals interested in employment opportunities (Candidates - Coaches)
(b) legal or business representatives of sports clubs interested in hiring tennis/padel coaches (Sports Club Representatives)

2.3.  CANDIDATES

The categories of personal information we collect from or about candidates include:

(a) Contact information: such as title, name, address, phone number, email address, job title.
(b) Basic information: such as the date of birth, age, marital status, interests, preferences, and favorites.
(c) Professional information: such as professional credentials, educational and professional history, and institutional affiliations.
(d) Communications data: such as information you provide in emails, phone calls, market research surveys or other correspondence with Racquet-Link or its service providers or business partners.
(e) Athletic resume, professional resume, presentation videos, profile pictures, on-court skill videos.

2.4.  SPORTS CLUB REPRESENTATIVES

The categories of information we collect from or about Sports Club Representatives include:

(a) Contact information: such as title, name, address, telephone number, email address, job title, and organization name.
(b) Basic information: such as your start date, age, marital status, interests, preferences, favorites, location, sports and facility information.
(c) Registration information: such as details relating to a service or event for which you are registering or attending, including conferences and events.
(d) Communications data: such as information you provide in emails, phone calls, market research surveys or other correspondence with Racquet-Link or its service providers or business partners.

4. Who do we share your personal information with?

We share your personal information with the following categories of recipients:

4.1.  SERVICE PROVIDERS

(a) Racquet-Link shares personal information with our service providers, including:

  (i) Data storage and analytics providers;
  (ii) technology services and support (including email and web hosting providers, marketing and advertising technology providers, and email communications providers); and
  (iii) event planning organizations that help facilitate company programs.

(b) The legal basis for sharing such information is our legitimate interest in operating and improving our business efficiently. Racquet-Link has entered into appropriate agreements with service providers that prohibit them from using or disclosing your personal information except as authorized.

4.2.  SPORTS CLUB REPRESENTATIVES

We share personal information with clubs once the selection process has progressed and a cooperation agreement has been signed between Racquet-Link and the club. Therefore, a professional relationship has been established. This personal information is necessary for the clubs to make a decision and to analyze the profile of each proposed user. Each registered user is aware that he/she is sharing his/her data in order to find job opportunities in different clubs or sports centers. In order to qualify for such an opportunity, the club must see the personal information in the profile in order to arrange an interview.

4.3.  OTHER THIRD PARTIES

We may disclose information about you to other third parties, such as governmental and regulatory bodies (e.g., tax authorities), social security authorities, courts, law enforcement agencies, government agencies, and outside advisors acting as controllers (e.g., lawyers, accountants, auditors, etc.). Such transfers are made to comply with our legal obligations and for the legitimate purposes of Racquet-Link S.L. or a third party to ensure the protection of the rights and property of Racquet-Link S.L. and others, including:

(a) if we are required to do so by law, court order or legal process; or
(b) in response to lawful requests by governmental authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements; or
(c) to enforce Racquet-Link S.L.'s policies or contracts; or
(d) to collect amounts due to Racquet-Link S.L., or
(e) if we believe disclosure is necessary or appropriate to prevent physical harm or financial loss or in connection with an investigation or prosecution of suspected or actual illegal activities.


5. Data storage

Racquet-Link will only retain the personal information it receives as described in this Privacy Policy for as long as you use our services or as necessary to fulfill the purpose(s) for which it was collected, including to provide our products and services, resolve disputes, establish legal defenses, conduct audits, pursue legitimate business purposes, enforce our agreements, and comply with applicable laws.

We are committed to deleting and destroying your personal information when it is no longer needed for the purpose for which it was collected and when the statute of limitations for any applicable legal action has expired.


6. Your options

6.1.  MARKETING COMMUNICATIONS

You may receive periodic e-mails, newsletters, postal mailings or telephone calls from us with information about Racquet-Link S.L. or our business partners' products and services, or about upcoming special offers/events that we think may be of interest to you, if you have opted in to receive these communications. We will only send you these communications with your prior express consent or if you have previously subscribed to a similar service from us (where such consent is required by law). You may unsubscribe from these communications at any time at no charge.

Each commercial e-mail communication sent by Racquet-Link S.L. will include an unsubscribe button.

6.2.  RIGHTS OF DATA SUBJECTS IN EUROPE

This section applies only to data subjects residing in the EEA, the United Kingdom or Switzerland, who have certain rights under European data protection laws and regulations, as summarized below:

(a) to request access to their personal data;
(b) to request the rectification of inaccurate or incomplete personal data;
(c) to request the deletion of your personal data;
(d) to restrict the processing of your personal data;
(e) to object to the use of your personal data by us;
(f) where applicable, request the portability of your personal data;
(g) where you have consented to the processing of your personal data, withdraw your consent; and
(h) lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority.

6.3.  You may request us to exercise the above rights by contacting us using the details set out below. In each case, your rights will be subject to the restrictions set out in applicable data protection legislation. You can obtain more information about these rights and the circumstances in which they may arise in relation to our processing of your personal data by contacting us at info@racquet-link.com.

6.4.  If you have any complaints or concerns regarding our handling or processing of your Personal Data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Authority. In Spain, this is the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD), which you can contact at www.aepd.es.
