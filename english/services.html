Services

Racquet-Link simplifies and professionalizes the search and recruitment of talent by facilitating the connection between coaches and racquet projects, as well as offering consulting and support services for sports clubs.

RECRUITMENT SERVICES

We act as specialized headhunters in racquet sports, connecting coaches and professionals with job opportunities around the world. We analyze the profile of each coach and work with options that best match their skills and experiences. Our goal is to make the search and selection process simpler and more efficient.

What benefits do we offer to coaches?

  • Access to International Opportunities: We expand your professional horizons internationally with access to global opportunities.

  • Time and Effort Saving: We handle the job search, saving you time and effort, and connect you with opportunities that match your profile.

  • Confidentiality: We ensure the privacy of your personal and professional information throughout the entire process.

  • Professional Development: We offer advice and support for your professional development, assisting you with all necessary procedures for your hiring.

  • Continuous Support: We guide you through the process, facilitating the transition into your new job.

What benefits do we offer to clubs?

  • Quality Talent Selection: We ensure highly skilled candidates that match your needs, guaranteeing quality staff.

  • Time and Resource Savings: We manage the entire recruitment process, from searching, interviewing, and reference checking to hiring, saving you time and resources.

  • International Talent: We work with professionals from different parts of the world.

  • Specialized Knowledge: Our team consists of professionals with international experience and in-depth industry knowledge.

  • Confidentiality: We manage the search and selection in a discreet and confidential way.

  • 360º Integral Service: We offer consulting services for the success and improvement of clubs and projects.

  • Guarantee: We provide warranties in case one of the candidates does not work out, seeking a replacement at no additional cost.

Frequently Asked Questions:

  • How much does it cost to register?

    Free Registration. Joining our community of racquet sports professionals is completely free!

  • How much do clubs have to pay for us to find the staff they need?

    We only charge a fee when we have successfully linked a professional with an opportunity that fits their profile. We work with you to understand your needs and goals. The fee is based on the type of position, the level of difficulty and the amount of resources required to hire the right candidate.

  • How much does a coach have to pay for us to find him/her a job? Click here:

    Joining our Racket Talent Community is free. However, in order to provide you with personalized support, we offer additional services such as career management, assistance with paperwork and bureaucratic matters, among others.

    In our first call with you, we will clearly outline all of the additional services we offer, as well as any costs, fees or commissions associated with them. Our priority is to help you advance your career effectively and fairly.

  • Who can see that I am registered with Racquet-Link?

    Complete confidentiality: We respect our members' privacy. Both registered profiles and job postings are kept strictly confidential.

  • How long does it take to find a coach?

    From the time we receive the application, our team will take 2-3 days to contact you for an initial interview. Generally, it takes 2-3 weeks to introduce you to the selected candidates once the plan has been agreed upon. This may vary depending on the position and conditions.

  • Does the recruitment service have a warranty?

    Yes, the Racquet-Link team will agree on an agreement period with each client before starting the service. If a candidate hired by the client does not meet the job commitments or does not fit into the company during that agreed-upon time, we will present a new candidate at no additional cost.

  • What if I need a coach for a clinic or exhibition?

    We also have talent for special events. Register and let us know your needs during the registration process or email us at info@racquet-link.com.

  • I want a temporary padel/tennis coach

    Our talent database is composed of coaches looking for permanent opportunities, as well as coaches looking for temporary opportunities. When you register, we will search for the specific talent you need.

  • Why should I register on Racquet-Link?

    Online registration takes less than 5 minutes and gives you access to a large and specialized network. Highlight your club's needs or your skills as a coach and let us help you find the best option for you.

  • I would like to find a job abroad, can Racquet-Link help me?

    Yes, we can. We have national and international clients. Register and our team will help you find a job that matches your profile and preferences.

  • Can you help me find a summer job?

    Yes, we have clients looking for staff to fill positions during the summer months. Additionally, we offer our career management services to assist you during the process.

  • How does Racquet-Link find me the right job?

    When our clients present us with a new job opening, we search our talent community for suitable candidates. We will contact you immediately if your profile matches the vacancy.

  • I have registered to find a job. When will you contact me?

    Our team will contact you as soon as possible. We will notify you immediately when we have an offer that matches your profile.

  • Why don't I see the club's offers directly?

    Among other reasons, because our services are confidential. Additionally, our clients use our services to reduce their workload. We take responsibility for pre-selection so that the company does not directly receive applications. This is only possible if the client remains anonymous during this phase.

  • How do you evaluate if a candidate is good?

    Our team meticulously reviews each profile. We conduct direct interviews to assess technical skills, experience, and personal aptitudes. We also verify references when necessary. We want to ensure we offer the best match between what our clients are looking for and the skills of our candidates.

  • Is it possible that the candidates we present have already been contacted by the client? What is the solution?

    Before we start, the client provides us with a list of candidates they have already contacted. They also inform us of any further contacts during the selection process. We maintain close communication throughout the process to ensure efficient management.

  • Can you offer me opportunities that I have already applied for?

    No. Prior to the start of the process, the candidate will provide us with a list of the companies he/she has already contacted. They also inform us of any other contacts during the selection process. We maintain close communication throughout the process to ensure efficient management.

  • I tried to create an account, but I received an error message saying that the email address I was using had already been registered.

    This email address is already in use. Try logging in again, making sure you are using the correct email and password, or try creating a new account using a different email address.

  • I forgot my username; how do I log in?

    Your username is always the email address with which you registered.

  • How can I update my profile data?

    When logged in, click on the settings and edit button to update your data.

  • I want to delete my data from Racquet-Link

    Please send us an email to info@racquet-link.com.

  • I forgot my password; how can I get a new one?

    Click on "Forgot your password?" on the login page, which will take you through all the steps to reset your password.
